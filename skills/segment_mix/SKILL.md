Use `get_segment_mix`.

Rules:
- Segment mix must use `vw_segment_stay_night`.
- Use `effective_macro_group`, not only the static `market_code_lookup.macro_group`.
- Shares must use the same filtered denominator for every segment.
- Room-night share and revenue share can tell different stories.

Interpretation:
- High revenue share with lower room-night share suggests stronger ADR/quality.
- High room-night share with lower revenue share suggests lower-rated base demand.
- Corporate and MICE may be useful base, but concentration risk should be checked.
- Retail/OTA mix should be evaluated for dependency risk if share is high.

Final answer:
- Name the top segments.
- Quantify room-night and revenue share.
- Explain whether the mix is healthy, risky, or opportunity-led.
- Recommend one next action.
