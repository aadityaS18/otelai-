---
name: commercial_recommendation
description: Use when the GM asks what action to take, what risk matters most, or how to respond commercially.
---

Combine outputs from safe tools; never invent numbers.

Decision rules:
- If demand is strong and high-rated: protect rate, reduce discounting, and avoid opening extra low-rated inventory.
- If demand is weak for near-term stays: use targeted direct campaigns before broad discounting.
- If OTA share is above 40%: evaluate dependency risk before adding more OTA exposure.
- If block share is above 45%: review displacement and concentration risk.
- If pickup is concentrated in one company or segment: monitor cancellation risk and avoid over-interpreting it as broad demand.

Answer format:
- Key number.
- Main driver.
- Risk or opportunity.
- Recommended next action.
- Assumption used.
